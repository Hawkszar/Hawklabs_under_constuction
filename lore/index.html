Universe Snapshot

Borders fail. Comms go dark. Invasion succeeds through underground access and complacency decay.

The North (Hawk Homeland)
Near-utopian nature-tech harmony. Disciplined but humane. Hawks are principled, not naive.
The West (Crow Undercities)
Depleted surface, massive under-network. Crows are brutal, not mindless — united by El Hefe.
The Cutoff
Home signal: none. Civil net: silence. Secure channel: dead.
Commander Hawk
Enhanced operator. Squad lead. Honorable to the core. Carrying secrets tied to black-ops history.
