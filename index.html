Universe Snapshot

The North (Hawk Homeland)

Near‑utopian, nature‑tech harmony, disciplined but humane. Hawks are principled, not naive.

The West (Crow Undercities)

Depleted surface, massive under‑network. Crows are brutal, not mindless — united by El Hefe.

The Cutoff

Comms go dark. Borders fail. Invasion succeeds via underground access and complacency decay.

Game: Lost Contact

Vertical‑scroller air‑to‑air combat. Fast missions. Clean reads. Real stakes.

Commander Hawk

Squad lead. Enhanced operator. Still capable of sustained flight when most cannot. Honorable to the core — and carrying secrets tied to a government black‑ops program.
