Universe Snapshot

Birds of War is a stylized military action universe: games, shorts, lore drops, and future media all linked back to one canon.

The North
Hawk homeland. Clean power, nature-tech harmony, farms, forests, mountains, and strongholds.
The West
Crow homeland. Depleted surface, hidden undercities, dirty fuel, and a united revenge machine.
The Drift
A roaming magnetic anomaly that causes Compass Kill, bad routes, false confidence, and dead channels.
Commander Hawk
Principled, enhanced, and cut off from command. His advantage is clarity under pressure.
