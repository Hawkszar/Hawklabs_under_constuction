Birds of War // Primer

Universe Primer

Birds of War is a modern humanoid avian military-action universe. The Hawk homeland in the North has built a clean, principled society around nature-tech harmony, strong communities, and disciplined defense. The peace breaks when the Crow West, believed broken for twenty years, returns with organization, underground access, and a hunger for the North's resources.

Core Tone
Military clarity inside stylized chaos. Real stakes, clean silhouettes, no magic.
Core Conflict
Duty vs survival, honor vs pragmatism, and the cost of getting comfortable after victory.
The Blitz
Crows strike through decay, underground routes, blackout pressure, and weather cover.
Public Rule
The story starts with Lost Contact. The final demo events remain classified.
