Commander Hawk

Commander Hawk is the playable lead of Lost Contact: principled, enhanced, and cut off from command during South recon. He does not panic when every channel goes dead. He reads the silence as a threat and turns north.

Code
Honorable to the core. No lying, cheating, or misleading.
Edge
Classified flight tech, elite reflexes, and an amber command signature.
Mission
Break the intercept and reach the truth of what happened in the North.
Lost Contact BriefLore Hub
Amber belongs to Hawk. The cleanest light on the battlefield should be the hero.
